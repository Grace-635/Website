The Three Little Piggies

Once upon a time, there were three little piggy brothers. They had just moved out of their parents house and neeeded houses of their own.

The first brother decided to use hay to build his house and in the next two days he was done. The second brother decided to use sticks to build his house and in the next four days he was done, but the third brother decided to use a material far stronger than the other two brothers, he used bricks and after working hard for two weeks, his house was done

One day a big bad wolf got hungry and decided to come eat the pigs, he went to the first brothers house and said little pig little pig open up the door and the little pig said not by the hair on my chinny chin chin then i'll huff and puff and blow your house down said the big bad wolf, and with a huge puff he blew the first brothers house away. The scared little pig ran away to his brothers house

The big bad wolf went to the second brothers house and said the same thing little piggies little piggies open up the door,not by the hair on our chinny chin chin, the piggies replied. And once again he huffed and he puffed and he blew the stick house away. And the scared little piggies ran away to their brother's house

In the third brothers house, the little piggies trembled with fear, and one again the big bad wolf came, repeating the same line little piggies little piggies open up the door not by the hair on our chinny chin chin, the piggies replied once more, and the big bad wolf took a deep breath and blew with all his might but the brick house remained intact, once again he blew and blew and blew, until he wore himself out.
